Additional Help

If You're a Victim

  • Stop all contact with the scammer
  • Preserve all evidence (messages, emails, etc.)
  • Report to your bank/financial institution
  • File a police report
  • Monitor your credit and accounts
  • Consider placing a fraud alert on your credit
