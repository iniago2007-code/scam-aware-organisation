Quick Warning Signs

⚠️

Requests for Personal Information

Legitimate companies never ask for passwords, SSN, or banking details via email or phone.

⚠️

Urgent Action Required

Scammers create pressure by claiming your account will be closed or action is needed immediately.

⚠️

Too Good to Be True

Offers promising easy money, prize winnings, or incredible returns are usually scams.

⚠️

Payment Requests

Be wary of requests to pay fees upfront for loans, jobs, or prizes you've supposedly won.

⚠️

Suspicious Links or Attachments

Never click on links or download attachments from unknown sources.

⚠️

Grammar & Spelling Errors

Professional companies proofread communications; many scam emails contain obvious errors.
